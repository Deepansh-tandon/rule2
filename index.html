UEFA AUCTION

1) Each participant team will have to acquire a club, a stadium, and a minimum number of 15 player.
2) There will be 200 players in this auction, of which of them will be auctioned off live on stage.
3) Teams must ensure that all the members are present at the auction desk if there’s an issue a single team member should be there at all times.
4) The managers will be assigned on the basis of the team you will buy.
5)All 200 player, stadiums, managers, and clubs will be auctioned via the auctioneer.
6) For players we will have 5 slots of 45 players each. The last round will be a bonus round for all the unsold player.
7) During the auction, the teams would have to raise their placards to show their interest and to keep the bidding process in continuation.
The total budget for each team will be 2.5 billion euros(Transfer Budget). There will be a master sheet maintained by the officials which will live track your team and the amount of money left with a group.
